Word Guessing Game

I developed a terminal-based, 2-player word guessing game where players will take turns to either choose a word or guess the other player's word. The game tracks each guess and indicates which letters are correctly placed and which letters are correct but in the wrong place.




Through this project, I strengthened my problem-solving skills while applying fundamental programming concepts and algorithmic thinking to create a functional, turn-based word-guessing game. I also focused on developing a user-friendly interface, ensuring smooth gameplay, clear prompts, and intuitive feedback for both players.
